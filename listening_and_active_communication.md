## 1. What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening? (Minimum 6 points)
* Focus your full attention on the speaker and the topic.
* Keep your personal opinions aside while listening.
* Pay attention to the speaker’s body language and facial expressions.
* Use encouraging responses (door openers) to continue the conversation.
* Paraphrase the content accurately to confirm understanding.
* Ask simple and non-threatening questions.
* Use acknowledgment responses like “yes”, “okay”, or nodding.

## 2. According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening?
* Try to understand the speaker clearly.
* Repeat or restate what the speaker said in your own words.
* Notice the speaker’s feelings, tone, and body language.
* Show that you are listening using small responses like “yes” or nodding.
* Ask questions if you need more clarity.
* Make sure both sides agree on the same meaning.

## 3. What are the obstacles in your listening process?
* Talking while others are speaking
* Asking people to repeat frequently
* Interrupting others during conversations
* Letting personal feelings affect listening
* Jumping to conclusions before the speaker finishes
* Not paying attention to body language and facial expressions

## 4. What can you do to improve your listening?
* Focus your full attention on the speake
* Set aside personal feelings and prejudices
* Observe body language and facial expressions
* Use encouraging phrases to continue the conversation
* Ask simple and clear questions
* Give short responses to show understanding

## 5. When do you switch to Passive communication style in your day-to-day life?
* When I don’t want to hurt someone’s feelings
* When I feel uncomfortable sharing my opinion
* When I think the other person may react negatively
* When I want to maintain peace and avoid conflict

## 6. When do you switch to Aggressive communication style in your day-to-day life?
* When I feel stressed or irritated
* When I feel disrespected
* When people interrupt or ignore me
* When I feel the situation is unfair

## 7. When do you switch to Passive-aggressive communication style in your day-to-day life?
* When I am upset but don’t want direct conflict
* When someone ignores my boundaries
* When I cannot express myself clearly
* When I expect others to understand without saying directly

## 8. How can you make your communication assertive?
* I speak clearly and directly about what I want or need.
* I use a calm tone and positive words instead of blaming others.
* I listen to the other person and respect their point of view.
* I set boundaries politely and stay confident without being rude.
